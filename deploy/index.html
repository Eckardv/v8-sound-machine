What We Do

Our Services

Everything your event needs to sound great, from the first mic check to the last song. We handle the setup, the equipment and the pack-down so you can enjoy the day.

Professional Event Sound

Crystal-clear audio for weddings, corporate functions, school events and private celebrations, sized to fit your venue just right.

Sound System Hire

Professional PA systems for intimate ceremonies and outdoor functions alike. We deliver, set up and run everything for you on the day.

Wedding Sound Specialists

Clear ceremony audio, wireless microphones for the vows and speeches, and reception music that keeps the celebration going. One team looks after your whole day.

Corporate Functions

Conference audio, presentations, awards evenings, year-end functions and product launches. Reliable, on time, and we invoice properly for procurement.

Wireless Microphones

Quality wireless microphones for speeches, vows and presentations, so every word reaches the back row without a hitch.

Event Lighting

Dance-floor lighting that gets the party started once the sun goes down.

DJ Entertainment

Young, energetic DJs who genuinely love what they do. Send us your playlist and your do-not-play list, and we'll take care of the rest.

Schools & Community

Dependable sound for school events, fun days and community gatherings. Clear announcements, family-friendly music and a setup that stays out of the way.

Custom Event Packages

Every event is different. Tell us about yours and we'll put together sound, lighting and entertainment to fit your venue and budget. Setup and pack-down always included.
