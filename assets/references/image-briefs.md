# V8 Sound Machine — Reference Image Briefs (Higgsfield / GPT Image 2)

Three 16:9 reference images for the landing page, later used as style anchors for the cinematic background video. Derived from `brand-kit.md` (July 2026).

**Model settings (all three):** GPT Image 2 · quality **high** · aspect **16:9** · photorealistic.

## Shared style block (prepend to every prompt)

> Cinematic premium product photography. Near-black stage environment (#0a0a0a). Lighting: a single magenta key light (#ff2da4) from high above like a stage spotlight, a soft violet-purple fill (#8b3dff) rising from below as a footlight, and a faint cool blue (#27a6ff) rim/edge accent. Subtle atmospheric haze catching the light beams, fine 35mm film grain, gentle vignette. Polished chrome and dark metal materials with neon reflections. Mood: chrome and horsepower meets stage light — a premium machine, quiet confidence, dark-luxury. No people, no text, no lettering, no logos, no brand marks anywhere.

**The consistent "product" across all three images:** a professional event sound rig — a matte-black PA loudspeaker cabinet with a polished chrome grille and precision-machined metal detailing, paired with a dark DJ controller with chrome faders and glowing accent knobs. Same rig, same finishes, in every image.
