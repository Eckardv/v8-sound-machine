## 1. Setup path (15 minutes)

1. Sign in at **business.google.com** with the Google account you want to own the profile (use a business account, not a personal one you might lose).
2. "Add your business" → name: **V8 Sound Machine**.
3. Business type: tick **Service business** only (you go to customers — no storefront).
4. When asked for an address: enter your real Wellington address, then choose **"I deliver goods and services to my customers"** and **hide the address**. Google requires the address for verification but won't display it — correct setup for a home-based business.
5. Set the service areas from §3.
6. Verification: for service-area businesses in ZA this is usually **video verification** — have your branded equipment (speakers, lighting rig, flight cases) and a business document (bank letter / invoice with the Wellington address) ready to show on camera.

## 8. Q&A — seed it yourself (allowed and recommended)

Post these as questions from any account, answer from the business account (adapted from the site FAQ):

1. **How do I book and secure a date?** → Send a booking request via v8sm.co.za or email info@v8sm.co.za. We confirm availability within 24 hours; a 50% deposit secures your date, balance due 7 days before the event.
2. **Which areas do you serve?** → Based in Wellington, we cover a 200 km radius: Paarl, Stellenbosch, Cape Town, Worcester, the Winelands and surrounds. Travel is quoted upfront.
3. **Can we choose our own music?** → Absolutely — send a playlist, a must-play list and a do-not-play list. Our DJs blend your choices with reading the room.
4. **What if the venue has no power?** → Our generator add-on comfortably runs the full sound and lighting rig.
5. **What happens if we cancel?** → More than 30 days out: full deposit refund. Within 30 days: deposit transfers to a new date within 6 months.
6. **How early do you set up?** → We arrive 2–4 hours before the event and complete a full sound check before your first guest walks in.

## 9. Reviews

- **Get the link:** GBP dashboard → "Ask for reviews" → copy the short `g.page/r/...` link.
- **Use it:** add it to the post-event thank-you email, and to the CRM app's follow-up flow (the app already drafts thank-you messages — add the link there).
- **Reply to every review within 48h.** Templates:
  - *Positive:* "Thank you, [name]! Powering your [wedding/party/function] at [venue] was a pleasure — enjoy the memories, and we hope to see you on a dance floor again soon. — V8 Sound Machine"
  - *Negative:* "Thank you for the honest feedback, [name]. This isn't the standard we hold ourselves to. Please email info@v8sm.co.za so we can make it right." (Take it offline; never argue publicly.)
